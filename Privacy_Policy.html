Privacy Policy — CodeCamp

Effective date: September 6, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how CodeCamp (the "App", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects information when you use our mobile application and related services. By using CodeCamp you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account & Profile: name, email address, profile picture, college/branch, year of study (if you create an account).
  • Resume & Career Data: resume/CV details you enter or upload for resume-building features.
  • Quizzes & Progress: answers, scores, course completion data, bookmarks and notes.
  • Support & Feedback: messages you submit through contact or feedback forms.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device & Usage Data: device model, operating system, app version, log files, crash reports, timestamps, and pages/screens visited.
  • Analytics: anonymized analytics about how you use the app (e.g., feature usage, time spent, interactions).

1.3 Third-Party & Integrated Services

When you choose to use third-party integrations (for example, Google Sign-In, payment providers, cloud storage, or analytics services), we may receive information from those providers as permitted by their APIs and your settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the App's features (learning content, quizzes, resume builder, calculator, AI assistance).
  • To personalize your experience — recommend courses, track progress, restore your data across devices.
  • To communicate with you about updates, offers, and important notices.
  • To analyze usage and improve the app's performance, features, and content.
  • To detect, prevent and respond to fraud, abuse, technical issues, or security incidents.

3. AI Features & Generated Content

If you use AI-powered tools (chat assistant, automated suggestions, code/help generation), we may process the input you provide to generate responses. You should avoid sharing sensitive personal data in free-text prompts. AI inputs and outputs may be logged for quality improvement and debugging unless you disable this in-app (if such an option is provided).

6. Cookies & Local Storage

We use cookies, local storage, and similar technologies to store preferences, session tokens, and small amounts of user data on your device. You can clear app data or uninstall the app to remove locally stored data.

8. Data Retention
