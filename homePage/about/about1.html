About CodeCamp

All-in-one learning hub

CodeCamp is a final-year engineering project turned full learning platform. It brings structured courses, notes, quizzes, aptitude prep, a resume builder, an AI study assistant, and even a scientific calculator into one focused app—so students can learn, practice, and prepare for placements without jumping between tools.
